# Compact Recovery Note Template

Use after context compaction or session handoff.

```text
COMPACT_RECOVERY_NOTE
PRODUCT_NAME=
CURRENT_GOAL=
SESSION_ROLE=Advisor | CEO | Reviewer | Builder | Unknown
WORKFLOW_LEVEL=LIGHT | MEDIUM | HEAVY
DECISIONS_MADE=[]
FILES_CHANGED=[]
FILES_TO_VERIFY=[]
OWNER_GATES=[]
NEXT_ACTION=
DO_NOT_DO=[]
```

Keep it short enough for the next agent to resume without re-reading the whole history.

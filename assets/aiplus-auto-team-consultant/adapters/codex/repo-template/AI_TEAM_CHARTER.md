# AI Team Charter

This repository may use AiPlus Auto Team Consultant to choose expert lenses and workflow depth during a Codex session.

## Roles

- Advisor: recommends and hands off.
- CEO: decomposes, dispatches, integrates, and verifies.
- Reviewer: finds blockers and risks.
- Builder: implements scoped work and hands off evidence.

The current agent remains responsible for scope control, verification, and Owner-gated actions.
